This is my local taquería, and I love it! The five tacos with charro beans are a great deal, and I especially enjoy the carnitas and the adovada. Great enchiladas, good breakfast burritos (I prefer them to Santiago’s), and the green chili is on point. They also have agua frescas, the horchata and the jamaica are my favorites. Fast, friendly service, and Ramon the owner is delightful. They recently added a patio, which is a lovely place to eat. There are a lot of choices for Mexican food in Longmont, this is one of the better ones!
