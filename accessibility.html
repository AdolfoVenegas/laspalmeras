Accessibility Statement for

is committed to making our site accessible to people with disabilities. We are continuously gathering feedback to improve the user experience for everyone.

Conformance

To provide a quality experience to our users, we follow the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG), which have three levels of accessibility: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. We have chosen level AA as a target for our website and we are fully committed to monitoring our site daily to keep this level of conformity.

Third-Party Vendors

Even though we cannot control their content, we encourage our third-party vendors (online orders and delivery providers, reservations and event management, social media, review sites, travel recommendation sites, etc.) to provide accessible and user-friendly content.
